FAQs

Find answers to common questions about ULLU Zero products and their functionality.

How does ULLU Zero work?

ULLU Zero works by using a combination of a physical device that can be plugged into a car's USB port and a smartphone app. It opens gates and garages based on user location and access status. Users can also share access with friends and family, and it can facilitate access for emergency services and autonomous vehicles.

What are the features?

ULLU Zero offers features such as auto open and close for gates and garages, secure access sharing, comprehensive control over access points, and the ability to facilitate access for autonomous vehicles.

Can autonomous vehicles use ULLU Zero?

Yes, ULLU Zero enables autonomous vehicles to enter gated neighborhoods without manual intervention. This helps overcome the current limitation of autonomous vehicles not being able to access gated areas.

What is the price range?

ULLU Zero offers four packages: Ullu Car for $60, Ullu Garage for $75, Ullu Garage Deluxe for $150, and Ullu Complete for $249. Each package comes with different features and capabilities.
